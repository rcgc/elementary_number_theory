4.3 LCM
. . .

Definition

The least common multiple of positive integers n1, n2, n3, ..., ni is a number, which is is a multiple of all given integers and is minimal.

Pseudocode

// Will return the lcm of a and b

PROCEDURE LCM( a , b ):
lcm = ( a * b ) / GCD( a , b )
RETURN lcm
