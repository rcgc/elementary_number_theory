4.1 GCD
. . .

Definition

Given a and b, we call d the greatest common divisor of a and b, denoted gcd(a,b), if:
  1. d | a and d | b.
  2. d is the greatest number that divides both a and b.


Pseudocode

// Will return the greatest common divisor of a and b

PROCEDURE GCD( a , b ):
IF( a == 0 ):
RETURN b
RETURN GCD( b % a , a )
